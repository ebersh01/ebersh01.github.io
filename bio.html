The Life of Ethan Bershtein

On a brisk winter evening, Ethan Bershtein was born in the Yale New Haven Hospital. He began his life in North Haven, CT but eventually moved to Woodbridge where he attended elementary school. During elementary school he distinctly remembers enjoying competitive games of kickball and foursquare during recess.

As time went on, Ethan transitioned to highschool. When realizing his natural athleticism and skill level was not going to carry him to a successful highschool basketball career, he decided to pursue his tennis career. He rose to #1 singles and won a state championship with his team.

Ethan was accepted to Tufts University, which he currently attends. He is in the beginning of his junior year, planning on majoring in Quantitative Economics and minoring in Computer Science. He is currently enrolled in Comp20, his new favorite class. He also is the captain of the Tufts Mens Tennis team.
